Our Vetting Process

Only the Best of the Best Wear the SAP Shield.

Every SAP guardian has earned their designation through a comprehensive vetting process. The institutions we serve — and the people inside them — deserve nothing less.

Note: SAP does not provide security training. We recruit professionals who are already trained, disciplined, and mission-ready — and we verify that their credentials are real.
  • VET / 01

    Honorable Discharge Verification

    We confirm every military guardian left service with honor.

  • VET / 02

    Federal Background Check

    Full national criminal history review.

  • VET / 03

    Sex Offender Registry Check

    National database. No exceptions.

  • VET / 04

    Drug Screening

    Held to the highest standard.

  • VET / 05

    Document Authentication

    Every credential verified.

About Us

Built by Those Who Served.

Safety Alert Patrol was founded on a simple belief: the men and women who dedicated their lives to protecting America should never stop serving — and America's schools, churches, and medical facilities should never go unprotected.

Our mission is to connect America's schools, houses of worship, and medical facilities with the best-qualified, most thoroughly vetted retired military veterans and law enforcement officers available — at no cost to anyone we serve.

Every dollar given goes directly to vetting, matching, and supporting the heroes who guard our most vulnerable communities.
