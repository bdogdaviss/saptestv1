Our Vetting Process

Only the Best of the Best
Wear the SAP Shield.

Every SAP guardian has earned their designation through a comprehensive vetting process. The institutions we serve — and the people inside them — deserve nothing less.

The SAP Vetting Standard

Five Checks. No Exceptions.

  • Honorable discharge verification — we confirm every military guardian left service with honor
  • Federal background check — full national criminal history review
  • Sex offender registry check — national database, no exceptions
  • Drug screening — held to the highest standard
  • Document authentication — every credential verified
Why This Matters

Trust Is the Whole Point.

A guardian's presence is only as valuable as the trust behind it. SAP's vetting process exists to protect the institutions and communities we serve — and to ensure that every guardian who wears our designation has earned it.
