Who This Is For

Done With Active Duty. Not Done Serving.

  • Honorably discharged U.S. military veterans
  • Retired law enforcement officers
  • Former first responders with security experience
  • Professionals who are done with active duty — but not done serving
What SAP Offers You

Purpose. Placement. The Shield.

  • A free platform to register your service, skills, and availability
  • Meaningful placement opportunities in schools, churches, and medical facilities
  • The SAP Guardian designation — earned through rigorous vetting
  • No cost to join. No fees. Ever.
  • The chance to keep doing what you were built to do — protect people
Our Vetting Standard

Already Trained. Already Proven. Verified Rigorously.

SAP does not offer training — we recruit professionals who are already trained, already proven, and already committed. What we do is verify it rigorously:

  • Honorable discharge verification
  • Federal background check
  • Sex offender registry check
  • Drug screening
  • Document authentication
